Q3:
where can I walk around a historic medieval city center?
A3:
You can walk around a historic medieval city center in 's-Hertogenbosch (Den Bosch), specifically in its well-preserved medieval centre, as described in [Source 5: 's-Hertogenbosch (Introduction)]. Additionally, Aarhus also has a historic city center with many historic buildings and preserved environments that can be explored on foot, such as the Latin Quarter in [Source 2: Aarhus (See)].

In 's-Hertogenbosch, you can walk along the winding streets to see Saint John's Cathedral and visit the market square. You can also explore the old fortifications by walking northwards via certain streets and bastions, as described in [Source 4: 's-Hertogenbosch (See)].
